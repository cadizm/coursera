It would seem there is a typo in section 1.3.2 "Vectorizing the gradient" in the series of entries demonstrating how to compute the partial derivatives for all θj where hθ(x)−y is defined.

The last row in the array has hθ(x(1))−y(m) but I believe this should be hθ(x(m))−y(m)
